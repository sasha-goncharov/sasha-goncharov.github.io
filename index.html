Alexander Goncharov

I am interested in several areas of Mathematics and Mathematical Physics:

  • Arithmetic Algebraic Geometry: L-functions, mixed motives and motivic Galois groups, polylogarithms, periods, Hodge Theory, and other topics.
  • Cluster varieties and Representation theory
  • Geometry, Quantum higher Teichmuller theory, Quantum Field Theory
  • Integral Geometry and Tomography
